Maggie Simpsons

Margaret Lenny "Maggie" Simpson is the youngest child of Marge and Homer Simpson, the baby sister to Bart and Lisa Simpson and one of the five main characters of The Simpsons. She is often seen sucking on her pacifier, and when she walks, she usually trips over her clothing and falls on her face.

English Voice Actor: Yeardley Smith
